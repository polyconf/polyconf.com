POLY CONF x LONDON

When? May 9th, 2016 Where? Campus London 3rd floor, Bonhill Street, London

PolyConf Satellite x London meeting is about bringing together people interested in polyglot approach to programming.

We will compare and contrast three major JavaScript frameworks (or libraries) i.e. Angular 2, EmberJS and React. There will be three presentations (one for each framework) followed by a panel discussion.

Speakers

An Introduction to Ember
Jamie White
React with no react
Andrei Preda
