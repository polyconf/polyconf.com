POLYCONF x WARSAW

When? March 15th Where? Campus Warsaw, Ząbkowska, Warszawa, Polska

PolyConf satellite meetings are about bringing together people interested in polyglot approach to programming with the aim to promote local companies and communities. Those meetups are held in various European cities with the support of local companies. Each meetup will have 3-4 talks (max 20 minutes each) followed by a networking session.

We are happy to have CodiLime, 10Clouds, EL Passion and Nukomeet on board!

Our speakers

Krzysztof Kwapisiewicz - "Goroutines and channels - simple, but powerful tools in Go arsenal"
Piotr Kilbert - "Polyglot web scraping or how to connect Python and Elixir"
Kasper Kondzielski - "Livecoding: evolution of languages on JVM"
