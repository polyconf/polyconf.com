POLY CONF x KRAKOW

When? April 20th, 2016 Where? hub:raum Krakow, Przemysłowa 12 30-701 Kraków

PolyConf satellite meetings are about bringing together people interested in polyglot approach to programming with the aim to promote local companies and communities. Those meetups are held in various European cities with the support of local companies. Each meetup will have 3-4 talks (max 15 minutes each) followed by a networking session.

We are happy to have Erlang Solutions, hub:raum and Nukomeet on board!
